Privacy & Deployment

Privacy-conscious by design, practical by deployment model.

  • De-identification is performed in real-time on live video.
  • Raw video is never stored or recorded, preserving privacy.
  • Faces are heavily block-pixellated ensuring they cannot be recovered.

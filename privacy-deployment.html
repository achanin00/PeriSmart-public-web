Privacy & Deployment

Built to reduce privacy risk before the cloud conversation starts.

PeriSmart approaches privacy through architecture and deployment choices: real-time de-identification at the edge and a practical integration model built around systems hospitals already use.

Privacy-First Overview

What matters most.

  • Faces and sensitive visible elements are de-identified in real-time on live video.
  • Raw video is never stored or recorded, preserving privacy.
  • Raw audio is never stored or recorded, preserving privacy.
Deployment Practicality

How deployment stays practical.

  • PeriSmart is designed around edge processing and outbound connectivity.
  • AI processing is done in real-time inside the hospital ensuring high performance while preserving privacy.
Key Messages

Concrete claims build trust faster than generic assurances.

Edge de-identification

Faces and identifiable features are removed on the edge device before video ever leaves the OR.

De-identified retention

Retained video is fully de-identified and used solely to support operational visibility, workflow review, and continuous improvement.

Integration flexibility

PeriSmart imports scheduling and planning context from the EHR and OR management systems your hospital already uses — no duplicate data entry required.

Operational focus

PeriSmart is built for perioperative coordination — streamlining room turnover, case scheduling, and team readiness, not patient monitoring or clinical decision-making.
